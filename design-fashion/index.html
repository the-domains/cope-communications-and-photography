Inspiring Interiors Photographed By Filippo Bamberghi - D'Marge

Filippo Bamberghi knows a thing or two about stylish homes. The Italian photographer specialises in capturing interior design at its most inspiring, from dark and moody walls, to whitewashed industrial elegance, to strategically cluttered objets d'art. Bamberghi's work has appeared in Vogue Living Australia, Architectural Digest, Casa Vogue Brasil, Elle Decor Japan, Interni, the Wall Street Journal and more.
